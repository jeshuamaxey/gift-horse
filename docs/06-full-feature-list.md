# Full Feature List

This document contains all features considered for the app, with MVP status indicated.

## MVP Features (Initial Release)

### Gift Idea Management
- ✅ Quick capture via image sharing from other apps (person assignment optional)
- ✅ Quick capture via in-app photo
- ✅ Quick capture via text entry form
- ✅ Gift states: Idea, Acquired, Gifted, Abandoned
- ✅ Gift associations: Can be unassigned, assigned to person, or assigned to person+occasion
- ✅ Manual state updates
- ✅ Gift details: Title, Price, Link, Notes, Image
- ✅ Image upload for gift ideas

### Person Management
- ✅ Create person (name only)
- ✅ Person list view with names and profile displays (initials or emoji)
- ✅ Optional emoji assignment per person (replaces initials in display)
- ✅ Person-specific gift ideas view
- ✅ Gift ideas grouped by upcoming occasions
- ✅ Gift ideas sorted by recency within groups

### Occasion Management
- ✅ Birthday tracking per recipient
- ✅ User-defined occasions (Christmas, etc.)
- ✅ Map recipients to occasions
- ✅ Birthday reminders (1 month before)
- ✅ Occasion reminders (customizable)

### User Experience
- ✅ Landing screen = Add Gift screen
- ✅ Archive view for gifted items (secondary access)
